```YAML

"type":mnist fashionmnist cifar10 cifar100
"local_epochs"
"batch_size"
"lr"
"momentum"
"optimizer":sgd adam rmsprop
"loss_function":cross_entropy mse nll
```

数据集固定为原来的0.2的数量

用户选择聚合方案无效，只有平均聚合

用户选择的优化器和损失函数无效，指定为SGD和cross_entropy

用户的选项：

"method":krum fedavg median pefl trimmed_mean shieldfl 
"global_epochs"
